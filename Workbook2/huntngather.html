Anders Hoff

Week 6

Interesting interactivity from Generative Unfoldings

Greed

Click here to visit Maja Kalogera's work.
Greed by Maja Kalogera was rendered using three.js, you can animate the terrain by pressing ‘m’, I like the animation of moving forward though the plains, it gives the viewer a bird’s view, at the same time the dark shadow from below and white haze from above gives it an eerie feel.

Hexells

Click here to visit Alexander Mordvintsev's Work.
Hexells by Alexander Mordvintsev is a Self-Organising System of cells, that was trained to build textures using neighbour communication only. This work exposes the relation between the life of an individual cell, and the cell collective. The work is interactive with the cells and surrounding cells changing colour at the swipe of your mouse.
